Henley Shirts

A Henley shirt is a collarless pullover shirt, characterized by a round neckline and a placket about 3 to 5 inches (8 to 13 cm) long and usually having 2–5 buttons. It essentially is a collarless polo shirt. a lightweight pullover shirt with a ribbed collar and a vertical opening at the neck fastened by a line of buttons.
